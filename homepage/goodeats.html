My Favorite Restaurants


Amaze Fusion & Lounge

1066 1st Ave., New York, NY 10022

This is one of my family's restaurant, and the one I work at during the weekends. My dad's the head chef!
Cuisine: Mostly Chinese food, but also some Thai, Vietnamese, and a sushi bar.
Reccomendations: ~Vietnemese Rice Noodles(a bunch of protein cooked with rice noodels and yellow curry)
~Shrimp Pad Thai
~Shrimp Pineapple Curry Fried Rice (Make sure to ask for baby shrimps)
~Bulldozer roll (Spicy scallop with yellowtail on top)
~Sushi pizza (Get this: a scallion pancake tooped with avocado, seaweed salad, fish of choice, roe, spicy mayo, and eel sauce)
~Salmon Don (a bento box with rice, seaweed salad, big chunks of fish, and pickeld daikons)

~Beef and Rice Cakes
~Seafood Rice Noodles
~Raw Conch Sashimi

Grand Dim Sum

Haidilao Hotpot

138-23 39th Ave, Queens, NY 11354

Cuisine:Shanghai Hotpot
Reccomendations, Besides the traditional hotpot items; ~Sweet Fried Mantou
~Shrimp Paste
~Hand Pulled Noodles (Done by a person in front of you)
~Spicy River Snail
~Handmade Fish Balls
